Fully Stocked

Container vessels are a common sight in ports around the world, but this one is particularly well-stocked.

They're a vital part of the global supply chain, but where is this one moored?

In which city would you find this port?

Ship markings

I see the ship has the markings "UASC". When I search for UASC and shipping, I see the United Arab Shipping Company.

The ship looks to be a UASC A15-Klasse or something similar.

I can see on top of the ship that there are cranes marked with the letters "RDKAI".

When I search for RDKAI cranes, an article from the Frankfurter Allgemeine newspaper comes up with images of cranes saying TERMINAL BUCHARDKAI.

It's hard to see whether the terminal is the same as the one in my picture, so I'll look on Google Maps.

When I search for Terminal Buchardkai on Google Maps, a port at Hamburg comes up.

I want to confirm this with more information, so I'll keep searching.

Port landscape details

The bridge in the background looks unusual, with the line of cranes in front of it.

When I search for this image using Google Image Search, a picture of the Hamburg port comes up with an article titled Hamburg Kick-Starts Elbe Expansion.

Zooming in on the image shows the bridge in the background.

I can also see the red cranes in the Hamburg port picture.

This confirms the earlier information.

There is probably a lot more searching I could do in more detail to confirm this further, but I don't think it's necessary.

The UASC ship is docked in the Hamburg port.

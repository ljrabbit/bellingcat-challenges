Challenge 1: Name that street!

Using OSINT to locate and identify a specific location

Challenge Overview

This challenge focused on locating a specific street based on various clues and using open-source intelligence tools to pinpoint the exact location.

Process & Findings

The investigation involved cross-referencing street names, landmarks, and digital footprints to establish the location. Using tools like Google Maps and street view, we identified the area.

Conclusion

This challenge proved to be an excellent exercise in OSINT techniques, demonstrating how accessible information can be pieced together to pinpoint a specific place with high accuracy.

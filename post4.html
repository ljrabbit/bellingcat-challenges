Challenge 4: Foul Play

This is what it looks like to be on the receiving end of a foul ball at a baseball game.

Be careful out there! But who is responsible for this hit?

What is the name of the batter?

This Bellingcat challenge shows a video clip of a batter hitting a ball in a baseball game. You can see the video here.

Uniform search

One of the best ways to identify a player in a game is to analyze their uniform.

Look for team logos, numbers, and other distinguishing features that can help identify the batter.

Stadium details

Stadium signs

On the top right, the ticker is displaying number 31 and a name which looks like it starts with D.

Baseball websites

When I search for baseballers who wear the number 31 in the year 2022, 2023 or 2024, a few players wear the number 31 and start with D.

The name "DeLoach" looks like it could fit.

When I Google 31 Deloach, I find that he plays for the Chicago White Sox.

I look up Blue Jays vs White Sox at the Rogers Centre, and find an image with uniforms that appear to match the uniforms in the video.

The name of the batter who hit the foul ball is Zach DeLoach.
